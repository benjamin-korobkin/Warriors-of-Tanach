# Warriors of Tanach

A fast, competitive digital card game built around characters from Tanach, with a primary focus on Shoftim and Melachim. Inspired by games like Marvel Snap.

You can play the game in-browser at https://korben12.itch.io/warriors-of-tanach
Also available to download for Windows, Mac, & Android.


# How to Play

2 players, 1 shared deck

Each player starts with 4 cards, including 1 random Prophet

The game lasts 5 turns

## Each turn:

- Players play 1 card 
    - Optionally, play your Prophet. But this can only be played *before* your power card

- Cards are revealed simultaneously (unless a Prophet changes this)

- Points are calculated based on power and conditions

- After 5 turns, the player with the highest total score wins

## Card Types

Kings

Generals - Can bolster or attack Kings

Shoftim - Increase in power with synergy

Prophets - introduce more tactical play
